Rules:
  1. Any live cell with two or three live neighbours survives.
  2. Any dead cell with three live neighbours becomes a live cell.
  3. All other live cells die in the next generation. Similarly, all other dead cells stay dead.
Control:
  • Press the Play/Pause button continue or pause the simulation.
  • The "Next" button will go-to the generation.
  • The "Clear" button is only available on the zeroth generation, and will remove all alive cells.
  • The "Reset" button is only available upon the first generation. It reverts the board back to the state of when the play button was initially pressed on generation 0.
  • Click on any rectangle to give life to a cell.
